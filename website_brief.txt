PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------------------------------------------------
This document serves as a comprehensive overview of the functionality delivered in the new Mode Men Magazine web platform. The system is a high-performance, custom-built application designed to deliver premium content, manage subscriptions, sell products, and serve targeted advertising.

1. PUBLIC FACING PLATFORM (USER EXPERIENCE)
----------------------------------------------------------

A. Content Experience
   - Dynamic Homepage: curated sections showcasing latest articles, interviews, and featured stories.
   - Comprehensive Content Types: 
     - Articles (Categorized with deep tagging)
     - Interviews
     - Events coverage
     - Sponsored content
     - Archive of Past Issues
   - Premium Reading Experience: 
     - Clean, distraction-free reading interface.
     - "Read Next" smart recommendations.
     - Progress saving and Reading History tracking.

B. Membership & Subscriptions
   - Tiered Access System:
     - Free User: Access to public articles with ads.
     - Premium Member: Unlocked exclusive content, reduced ads.
     - VIP Member: All access, ad-free experience, special perks.
   - User Accounts: Secure Login/Signup, Profile management, Subscription status view.
   - Content Gating: Smart paywalls that lock premium content for non-subscribed users while allowing previews.

C. E-Commerce (The Shop)
   - Product Catalog: Browse exclusive merchandise and magazine issues.
   - Shopping Cart: Full cart functionality.
   - Checkout & Orders: Seamless order processing system.

D. Advertising System
   - Smart Ad Injection: Ads are dynamically inserted into articles.
   - Contextual Targeting: Advertisements can be targeted to specific article categories (e.g., displaying luxury watch ads only in "Style" or "Business" articles).
   - Dedicated "Advertise With Us" portal to capture leads.

2. ADMIN DASHBOARD (CMS)
----------------------------------------------------------
The platform includes a powerful, custom-built Content Management System (CMS) giving full control over the website without needing a developer.

A. Content Management
   - Article Editor: Full rich-text editor, image uploading, and category tagging.
   - Manage all content sections: Interviews, Events, Past Issues, Sponsored Posts.
   - Categories Management: Create and edit content categories.

B. Shop Management
   - Product Management: Add/Edit/Remove products, manage pricing and images.
   - Order Management: View and process incoming customer orders.

C. Ad Server Management
   - Ad Campaign Manager: Upload creative assets (banners).
   - Targeting Logic: Assign tags to ads to control where they appear.
   - Performance: View active ads.

D. User & Audience Management
   - User Database: View registered users and their subscription tiers.
   - Newsletter: Manage subscriber lists for email marketing.
